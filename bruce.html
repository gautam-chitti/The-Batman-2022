BRUCE WAYNE

" BILLONAIRE "

The son of Thomas Wayne and Martha Wayne is a billionaire orphan. His parents were murdered by some assassin's of Carmine Falcone. He is not seen in public or his office usually. His butler Alfred is his father figure. His latest appearance was on the Funeral of the Mayor. We wonder what does he do with all his time.
