RIDDLER

" I am Vengence "

A serial killer who suffered in his childhood because he is orphan. He didn't get any facilities because of the corruption of Gotham city . He is master of riddles that's why he is called RIDDLER. He thinks that he is also a hero who is trying to erase the corruption for the City. He is a pyschopath. Luckily he was caught by GCPD with the help of THE BATMAN. Currently he is being held in the Arkham Prison.
